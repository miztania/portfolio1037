Projects

Mookratha Game

"Moo-kratha" is a party game that simulates the joy of eating Moo-kratha(grilled-pork) with friends by allowing players to compete for collecting good food and bring it to their dish. In my role as Director and Lead Programmer, I designed the game mechanics, managed tasks, and programmed the project in Unity and C# over a two-week development period with a team of 9 members.

Dance! Monkey

"Dance! Monkey" is a 3D platformer with rhythm-based gameplay, where players control a circus monkey dodging obstacles in sync with the music. Inspired by the exploitation of animals in circuses that being forced to perform dangerous acts for entertainment, the game uses Unreal Engine 5, helping us prepare for our next project, "Bungfai." As Game Director, Art Director, and Game Designer, I managed tasks, designed the game’s mechanics, and collaborated closely with the Programmers to ensure smooth gameplay. Our team’s efforts earned us 2nd Runner Up in the competition.

BUNGFAI : RISE OF THE NEW HOPE

BUNGFAI: RISE OF THE NEW HOPE is a 3D action-adventure game that reimagines Bun Bang Fai, the Isaan Rocket Festival Legend, with in-air combat as the core mechanic. Players use Bungfai(rocket) as weapons and tools while navigating through mythological Thai landscapes. As Game Director, I led the project from concept to completion, forming the team and managing development. In my roles as Art Director and Game Designer, I developed characters, environments, story, and gameplay systems, collaborating with programmers to achieve our vision. The project aims for national-level support, merging traditional culture with fast-paced, aerial combat gameplay.

Body&Soul

"Body&Soul" is a 2D Hack & Slash game that follows Marcus's quest to rescue his daughter from a demon, involving a unique mechanic for switching between the human and spirit worlds. I contributed as both Game Director and Lead Programmer, overseeing the project by managing tasks and handling core programming using Unity and C# over a month-long development period with a team of 9 members.

PaperGlider

"PaperGlider" is an online multiplayer mobile game where players navigate paper airplanes through a beautifully crafted world, focusing on maneuvering against wind currents and avoiding obstacles. As Lead Developer, I managed project planning and tasks, while also serving as Game Director, Game Designer, and Art Director, overseeing gameplay mechanics and visual elements. This project received 10,000 THB in funding support, showcasing our team's creativity and technical skills in the National Software Contest 2024.

Root Monster

"Roots Monster" is a short story, 2D top-down game about a mole’s quest to save his family from a rampaging root monster. Developed by my team of 2 during the competition, I contributed as the Game Developer by overseeing ideas, level designs, and programming in Unity with C#. Our teamwork led us to achieve the first runner-up award.
